Exporio Rebrand

Visual rebrand — brand identity & design system

Exporio is a fashion and lifestyle B2B platform helping smaller companies set up supply chains across the GCC region. It operates under Opptra, a venture by Binny Bansal — co-founder of Flipkart.

The brief was to build their brand from scratch: logo, website, and promotional video content. I was a core part of the logo construction and led the motion work, creating promo reels that introduced the brand to its target market.

Client Exporio / Opptra
Sector Fashion & Lifestyle B2B
