Pokedoro

Pokémon-themed pomodoro timer — built & shipped

Pokedoro is a pomodoro timer wrapped in a Pokémon skin — each focus session earns you a random encounter. Built as a fun side project to make deep work feel a little less like work.

Fully functional, deployed, and live. The design leans into the Game Boy aesthetic — pixel-adjacent, nostalgic, and immediately readable. Every interaction is intentional: the timer counts down, a Pokémon appears on completion, and you move on.
