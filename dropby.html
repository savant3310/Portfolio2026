Drop By

Brand guidelines & UI screens for a social discovery app

Drop By is a social discovery app built around spontaneous, location-based meetups — the digital equivalent of running into someone you know. The brief was to build a brand identity and UI system that felt casual and warm without losing the polish expected of a consumer app.

I developed the full brand guidelines and UI screens — covering logo, colour system, typography, components, and key app flows. The design language needed to feel approachable for everyday users while remaining consistent enough to scale.
