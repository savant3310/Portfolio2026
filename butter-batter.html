Butter & Batter

Bakery brand & digital presence — built & shipped

Butter & Batter is a boutique bakery specialising in assorted flavoured macarons. I handled their complete digital presence — website build and hyper-realistic 3D renders of their product lineup.

The renders were produced in Blender, styled to feel more editorial than commercial — clean backgrounds, precise lighting, and enough detail to make the macarons look genuinely edible on screen.
